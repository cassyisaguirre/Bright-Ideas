"I WANTED A BOOKSTORE BECAUSE THE BOOK BUSINESS IS THE BUSINESS OF LIFE"

We have remained close to the hearts of millions of people across generations for the past two years. Whether they are readers, artists, students, or the entire family, they will always find a home here in The Attic Library, no matter where they are in life. We are honored to be a constant in everyone's path, fostering their interests and inspiring their progress. Zarrick Amiel Mercadejas, our founder and CEO, exemplified hard labor, strong leadership, and persistence. It's not simple to start and think about business at such a young age, yet he managed to operate the firm successfully despite this. His story serves as an inspiration to individuals all across the world, as well as a reminder of the importance of perseverance.
